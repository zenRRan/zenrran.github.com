##### 1.sigmoid
函数：f(z) = 1 / (1 + exp( − z))
导数：f(z)' = f(z)(1 − f(z))
[sigmoid](https://github.com/zenRRan/zenrran.github.io/tree/master/images/sigmoid.png)
##### 2.tanh
函数：f(z) = tanh(z)
导数：f(z)' = 1 − (f(z))^2
[tanh](https://github.com/zenRRan/zenrran.github.io/tree/master/images/tanh.png)
